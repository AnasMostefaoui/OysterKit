ASTNodeConstructor

public protocol ASTNodeConstructor

ASTNodeConstructors are responsible for building the data-structures used by the default homogenous and heterogenous IntermediateRepresentations. They allow you to, by providing your own implementations, control the behaviour of Node creation of those default implementations.
